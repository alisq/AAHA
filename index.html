Urban Indigenous Housing on Stolen Land

—West Coast


Each team will develop a demand and architectural proposal that will be placed here.

Housing development designed by Patrick Stewart


This team will focus on the distinct and urgent needs of culturally appropriate and adequate housing for urban Indigenous people in Canada. The team will draw on traditional Indigenous knowledge to produce new morphologies, financial and social formations in housing.

Region: Northern Territories


Each team will develop a demand and architectural proposal that will be placed here.


Home is Land. In the north one can be house-less while still being “at home”. As economic development follows resource extraction throughout the region, where commodity prices remain higher than in other places, housing continues to be a major challenge. How can the intimate relationship to the Land offer a different path forward?

Region: Ontario


Each team will develop a demand and architectural proposal that will be placed here.

some caption text here


The team has experience supporting individuals living in tent encampments. It will design an architectural strategy that gives individuals living rough, or in tents, a foothold in secure housing, that can then provide a platform for housing evolution, allowing residents the agency to transform and expand their space to address their changing living situations.
